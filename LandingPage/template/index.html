ABOUT MANPOWER

The Las Piñas Manpower & Youth Council, a Vocational and Technical Training Center was established on April 16, 1982, through Executive Resolution led by the late former Mayor Filemon C. Aguilar and now known as Las Piñas Manpower Training Center.

MISSION

Center of excellence for qualaity technical and skills training by providing timely curriculum in the local and global tech-voc industry.

VISION

Develop globally competitive and higly competent members of the workforce through dynamic and updated skills training ultimately providing the best skills for high demand jobs.

MANDATE

Provide quality training to the Out of School Youth and Unemployed Adults of the City and to land a blue collar job and make livelihood to the uplift the lives of the Las Piñeros and to lessen the unemployment of the city.
